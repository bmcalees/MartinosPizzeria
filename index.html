Our Story

Welcome

Martino’s Pizzeria has been proudly serving the Webster/Penfield area for over 40 years! In January 2007, Martino’s was re-opened under the current ownership and management. Owner, Abel, strives to bring new ideas and flavors to the menu while also preserving the classic, old-world feel of the pizzeria and taste of the authentic, homemade Italian food. From our fresh made sub rolls and focaccia bread to homemade soups and pasta dinners to our award-winning pizza crust and sauce, we use fresh ingredients to make the tastiest food for our customers.

Place an order for take-out or delivery or stop by for lunch or dinner to taste the food yourself. You will be pleasantly surprised and satisfied!
